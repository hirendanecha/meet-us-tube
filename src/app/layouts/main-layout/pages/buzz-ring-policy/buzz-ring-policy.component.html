BuzzRing App Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BuzzRing we collects, uses, and shares information about users when you use our mobile application, BuzzRing App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect certain information when you create an account on our website, https://meetus.tube/, and use our services. This information may include:

  • A. Account Information: When you create an account on https://meetus.tube/, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • B. Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. This information helps us optimize the performance of the App and provide personalized content.
  • C. Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with our website, such as the features you use and the content you access.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • A. Account Integration: When you use the BuzzRing app, you have the option to integrate your account from https://meetus.tube/ by scanning a QR code generated from your profile. This allows us to provide personalized features such as call notifications within the app.
  • B. Communication: We may use your contact information to send you notifications, alerts, and updates related to the BuzzRing app and our services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with service providers who assist us in providing and maintaining the BuzzRing app.

4. Children's Privacy

The BuzzRing app is designed for users of all ages, including children who are 5 years old and above.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your information, including implementing appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Deletion

Users of the BuzzRing app have the option to delete their data by utilizing the delete icon within the app. This feature allows users to remove any information associated with their account from our servers.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated within the BuzzRing app or through other appropriate means.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or the BuzzRing app, please contact us at support@MeetUs.tube.
